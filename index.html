We are Feynic Technology


We are an emerging technology consultancy and tech ecosystem planning group that works with founders, investors and institutions to create sustainable growth of horizon technology. Our mission is to help lead the next wave of innovation.

Our goal...


  • is to explain clearly and concisely just how your business can focus its strategic planning and embrace emerging technologies in order to maximise efficiencies and income – equitably and sustainably.

  • is to help founders, investors and established institutions understand the changing directions of technology and how these impact their businesses so that they can make better informed decisions about where to allocate their resources.

  • is to open up opportunities and be an advocate for myriad different voices, using our expertise to network, build bridges and democratise tech.
